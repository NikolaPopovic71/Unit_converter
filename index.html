Welcome

to the converter for the most used units

Using the converter is very easy:

  1. enter the number to convert in the input field, the number could be up to 9 decimals (e.g. 34.123456789)
  2. choose number of decimals of your final result
  3. click one of the conversion button, and the result appears immediately
  4. click the "Reset" button to clear the input field

Notes

  • Jutro, lanac and dulum are specific Serbian area units
  • Here for dulum is used formula 1 dulum = 10 ar, but in some parts of Serbia (the surroundings of Leskovac) 1 dulum = 16 ar
  • 1 ar = 100 m2 (area 10 m x 10 m)
